WEST HILL MANOR

Thanks to the generous hospitality of Jan’s brother and sister-in-law who have opened their home to us, The JaNic’s wedding ceremony will take place on an historic and magical estate in Gates Mills, Ohio on October 3rd 2015.

West Hill Manor was built by industrialist and philanthropist Elroy J. Kulas in the early 1930’s. This Tudor Revival estate by famed Boston architect Charles R. Greco, together with its numerous outbuildings, was individually listed on the National Register of Historic Places on March 23rd 1988.
