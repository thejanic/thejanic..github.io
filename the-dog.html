Schnaps-C. (aka “Schnapsy Poo-Poo”) was born in Cypress, Texas – hence the “C” for his middle initial. This white Miniature Schnauzer is quite the character and loves to shred anything and everything as well as bark at strangers.

Schnaps is ever grateful to have been hand selected for adoption by Thor-Olav over his other 5 siblings. Thor-Olav got to bring his new little brother home when he was 5 weeks old. While things have since calmed down slightly, the first year and a half with a puppy were quite interesting and destructive. No toy – or anything resembling same – is safe from Schnapsy’s schnoz.
